# Not downloaded: Metropolis 1971 spectrum paper

Paper: N. Metropolis, "Spectra of determinant values in (0,1) matrices", in A. O. L. Atkin and B. J. Birch, editors, Computers in Number Theory: Proceedings of the Science Research Council Atlas Symposium No. 2 held at Oxford, 18-23 August 1969, Academic Press, London, 1971, pp. 271-276.

Relevance: BOOZ and the archived maxdet spectrum page cite this as the first computation of the n=8 determinant spectrum and its gaps. BOOZ states S_8 = {0,1,...,18,20,24,32} in normalized (0,1)-order-8 terms; through the standard (0,1) to {-1,1} correspondence this informs the small-order spectrum history.
