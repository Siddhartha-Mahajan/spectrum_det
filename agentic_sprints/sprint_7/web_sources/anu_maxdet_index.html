  • Lower bounds on maximal determinants
    (joint work with Judy-anne Osborn)
    We give lower bounds on the maximal determinant of n times n {+-1} matrices, both with and without the assumption of the Hadamard conjecture.
    Overhead transparencies (beamer format): [pdf]

Hadamard Workshop in Honour of Kathy Horadam, RMIT, Melbourne, 28 November 2011

  • Finding many solutions of the Hadamard maximal determinant problem given the maximal Gram matrices
    (presented at the International Workshop on Hadamard Matrices and their Applications in Honour of Kathy Horadam's sixtieth birthday, RMIT University, Melbourne, Australia, 28-30 November, 2011).
    We describe how randomised decomposition and switching can be used to find many solutions of the Hadamard maximal determinant problem for certain orders.
    Revised overhead transparencies (beamer format): [pdf]

The following links are relevant to the paper On minors of maximal determinant matrices.
